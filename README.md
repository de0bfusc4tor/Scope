## 𝐅𝐞𝐚𝐭𝐮𝐫𝐞𝐬

- **Atomic 3D Visualization**: A dynamic orbital model that translates pulses, steps, and repeats into a rotating geometric system.
- **Bi-directional MIDI Integration**: Automatic hardware detection with real-time monitoring of Control Change (CC) messages.
- **Zero Internet Dependency**: Runs entirely locally for maximum stability during live performances and studio sessions.
- **Hardware-Inspired UI**: Minimalistic, dark-themed dashboard including Bank/Pattern grids and a linear Step Monitor.
- **Precision Synchronization**: Latency-free visual feedback locked to MIDI Clock for perfect rhythmic alignment.
- **Technical Dashboard**: Instant readouts for BPM, Bank indices, and active Track parameters.

## 𝐑𝐞𝐪𝐮𝐢𝐫𝐞𝐦𝐞𝐧𝐭𝐬

- **macOS**: 14.0 (Sonoma), 15.0 (Sequoia), or 16.0 (Tahoe).
- **Architecture**: Intel & Apple Silicon.
- **Hardware**: Torso Electronics T-1 (connected via USB).

## 𝐈𝐧𝐬𝐭𝐚𝐥𝐥𝐚𝐭𝐢𝐨𝐧

1. Download the latest `Scope.app.zip`
2. Extract & Drag to your `Applications` folder.
3. Connect your `T-1` via USB.
4. Open `Scope`.

---

## 𝐈𝐧𝐭𝐞𝐫𝐟𝐚𝐜𝐞 & 𝐂𝐨𝐧𝐭𝐫𝐨𝐥𝐬

- **Banks**: 16-step grid for quick navigation across the `T-1`'s internal architecture.
- **Patterns & Tracks**: 16-step grid Visual of the active pattern and track index from the `T-1`.
- **Play, Stop & Sync**: Remote Play, Stop, and Global Sync status. Triggers the Start & Stop on your `T-1`.
- **Steps**: Linear view of the current sequence progress for micro-timing tracking.
- **Bank, PTN & TRK, BPM**: real-time monitoring of the selected Bank, Pattern & Track and BPM.
